# Assignmate — Features Guide

Everything you can do with Assignmate.

---

## 1. Your Account

Create an account with your email and password. Your handwriting profiles, assignments, and PDFs are all private to you — no one else can see or access them.

---

## 2. Handwriting Profile

Upload a single photo of your handwriting and Assignmate will clone your style. The AI reads your letter slant, spacing, stroke weight, line irregularities, and overall writing pattern to build a profile unique to you.

- You can have one active handwriting profile at a time
- Replace it anytime by uploading a new photo
- Your profile is reused automatically for every assignment you generate

---

## 3. Generate an Assignment

Type a question or a topic and Assignmate will write a complete, well-structured academic answer for you. The answer is generated by AI and written in a style appropriate for school or university submissions.

---

## 4. Handwriting Rendering

Your generated assignment is rendered in your own handwriting style on a realistic paper background. The rendering applies natural variation — slight random tilt, letter spacing differences, ink color simulation, and natural line flow — so the output looks genuinely handwritten, not typed.

**Paper styles you can choose from:**

- Lined notebook
- Plain white
- College-ruled

---

## 5. Customize Before You Export

Before downloading, you can adjust:

- Ink color
- Paper style
- Font size
- Line spacing
- Page margins

These settings are saved individually per assignment.

---

## 6. PDF Export

Export your assignment as a multi-page PDF. The PDF preserves your handwriting style, paper background, margins, and page layout exactly as previewed. A download link is provided instantly.

---

## 7. Assignment History

Every assignment you generate is saved to your account. From your dashboard you can:

- View all past assignments
- See the original question, date, and paper style used
- Re-download any PDF at any time

> PDFs are automatically deleted after **30 days** unless you mark the assignment as saved.

---

## 8. Preview Before Downloading

Before exporting to PDF, you get a live canvas preview of exactly how your assignment will look — paper background, handwriting style, margins, and all. Adjust any setting and the preview updates instantly.
